Association rules

\(Rule: X \implies Y\)

\[Support = \frac{freq(X, Y)}{N}\]

\[Confidence = \frac{freq(X, Y)}{freq(X)}\]

\[Lift = \frac{Support}{Supp(X)\times Supp(Y)}\]
